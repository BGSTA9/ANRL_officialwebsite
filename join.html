What We Look For

ANRL is built on the principle that great research comes from diverse minds united by obsessive curiosity. We value demonstrable skill, mathematical rigor, and the drive to build systems that advance our understanding of prime structures.

  • Strong computational skills (Python, or equivalent)
  • Experience with deep learning frameworks
  • Interest in number theory and algebraic structures
  • Working GitHub portfolio or equivalent open-source contributions
  • Self-directed research capability
  • Comfort with open science and public collaboration

Don't meet every criterion? If you're driven by genuine curiosity about the arithmetic structure of reality, we'd still love to hear from you.
